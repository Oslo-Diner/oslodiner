Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you deliver meals?
Yes, we have provisions to get your favourite meals to you at the convinience of your location. You order, we deliver. PS: We deliver to only Yaba and its environs for now. However, as our branches increase, so will our reach.

2. How long do deliveries take? Deliveries take between 30 minutes to 2 hours from when the order was made. We advise customers to order dishes ahead of time.

3. What is the price range of meals? Our meals are very affordable. Prices range from N1,500 to N8,000 per serving.

4. What does it cost to host events at Oslo diner? Oslo Diner has flexible packages fir events depending on the nature of the event and how much of our resources will be used. We charge per hour. For further enquiry, call 0802-802-4752.
