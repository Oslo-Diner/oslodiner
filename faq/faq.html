Q.Reservations

Parties less than 6 people are first come, first serve.
We accept reservations for 6 people or more and on Holidays only.
We apologize, but on nights where there is a wait for a table, all the guests in your group must be present before we can seat you.
Please call us directly if ​you need a reservation for 6 people or more. ​​
We do accept reservations on New Years, Valentines, Mother Day and Fathers Day for parties of all sizes.
Automatic discount of 18% is applied to parties of 6 or more.
