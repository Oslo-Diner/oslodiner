FAQ's

Reservations

Parties less than 6 people are first come, first serve.
We accept reservations for 6 people or more and on Holidays only.
We apologize, but on nights where there is a wait for a table, all the guests in your group must be present before we can seat you.
Please call us directly if ​you need a reservation for 6 people or more. ​​
We do accept reservations on New Years, Valentines, Mother Day and Fathers Day for parties of all sizes.
Automatic discount of 18% is applied to parties of 6 or more.

Parking

There is free street parking on both sides of the restaurant for all our guests. Our lot can park up to 25 at a go.

Priority Requests

Priority is given to hotels.ng customers within Nigeria.
Please understand we get a large number of requests and we are unable to fulfill every priority request.
If you wish to submit a priority request, please send us an email at:
oslodiner@casadelpaper.com
If we are able to fulfil your request, we will contact you to confirm your reservation.

Children's Menu

We do not have a children’s menu, however the chef can accommodate a child with a smaller portion on some of our dishes. It comes at a 20% discount.

Food Allergies

We offer a Gluten Free Menu for our guests with Gluten intolerance.
The chef can accommodate most vegan and vegetarian diets.
If you have a special diet, please call ahead to verify we can accommodate your specific needs.

Discount Cards

Sorry, we do not accept discount cards yet.

Take Outs & Delivery

We are happy to prepare take out but we do not deliver.
On the weekends however, please call at least one hour in advance for take out orders.

Holidays

We have a special menu for New Years Eve, Valentines and Mothers Day.
Please call for the menu selections and don’t forget to make your reservation.
We are CLOSED New Years Day, Easter, Thanksgiving, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.

Payments

We accept Cash and all major Credit Cards. Sorry, we do not accept any checks yet.
